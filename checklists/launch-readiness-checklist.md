# GTM Launch Readiness Checklist

A simple checklist used before launching a new product or feature. There are 5 key milestones 

- Market research & understanding
- Product Positioning
- Messaging
- Market testing
- Market readiness
- Internal launch and sales enablement
- Market activation
  - Digital launch
  - Event/field launch
  - Market activation assets - whitepaper, blogs etc
  - Campaigns and timelines
  - Analysts Relations
- Market observation/feedback loop
  

## Market research & understanding

- Market sizing - TAM 
- Key market segments defined
- Key competitors/SWOT
- ICP defined
- Key customer jobs defined
- Key customer pain points documented

## Product Positioning

- Customer pains based on jobs Vs our product gains -> clearly mapped
- 
- Value proposition defined and agreed
- Positioning defined and agreed
- Differentiation clear
- Competitive alternatives documented

## Messaging

- Messaging aligned with product team
- Messaging validated with sales

## Market testing

- Positioning and messaging aligned and validated with sales
- Test the messaging with customers/partners
- Iterrate as needed

## Market readiness

- Decide the readiness toolkit - some must-haves and some optional
  - Must-haves: Playbook, sales deck, webcopy
  - Optional: Demo video, explainer video, datesheet/one-pager etc.
- Prepare each of the must-have artifacts
- Review and sign-ogg
- Prepare each of the must-have artifacts
- Review and sign-off
- Store all the artefacts at a central repository

## Decide launch KPIs and create dashboards leveraging tools to monitor

- KPIS
  - Target for pipelines
  - Target for Revenue
  - Customer feedback on launch and assets
  - Sales feedback on launch and assets
- Tools
  - Create feedback for artefacts
  - Create sales force dashboard for launch
  - Ensure leads are loaded
  
## Internal launch and sales enablement

- Launch the new material with an internal comms email
- Annouce in other internal forums
- Set dates for sales enablement to go through launch package and key key of the launch
- Ensure the enablement is recorded and stored for future reference

## Market activation

- Digital launch
     - Decide channels for launch - LI, PR, partner channels along with dates, and owners
- Event/field launch
     - Decide how and where to launch the product - event (keynotes), roadshows etc
     - Requirements for these launches, dates, and owners
- Market activation assets - whitepaper, blogs etc
     - Map customer journey and decide the topics for discovery, awareness, consideration, purchase
     - Content creation along with contributors, reviewwers, approvers, and go-live dates.
- Campaigns and timelines
    - Decide if we want to do an organic or paid campaign
    - Map the cpre team, topics, decide KPIs, and timelines   
- Analysts Relations
    - Check if we want to do AR - who, when and budget

## Market observation/feedback loop

- Create core group for monitoring launch - comprises sales, product, product marketing, marketing
- Conduct sales and prospect interviews
- Create regular operating mechanism to meet and discuss progress
- Iterate as needed
